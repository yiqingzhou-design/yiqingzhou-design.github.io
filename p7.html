Zen.

Kinetic Installation
2022

Tool: Sketch, Adobe XD, Rhino, Cinema 4D

I want to create a kinetic installation that engages all the senses in order to give visitors a Zen experience. Based on Bruce Mau’s LIVE design theory and methodology, this project is designed to explore ways to convey emotions and feelings through various senses.
