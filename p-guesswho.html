Modified Game:

Guess Who

Game Design
Group Project | 2023

Tool: Illustrator

The original game of Guess Who is very straightforward. Players ask questions back and forth. Players ask questions back and forth. Once they have it figured, they can play it through quickly. To introduce more uncertainty & chance and to increase the challenge for players, we developed two types of card play to add to the game. Throughout our changes, we tried to stay true to the main construct of the game, while adding a deeper layer complexity.
