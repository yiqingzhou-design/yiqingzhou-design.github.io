Sany -
Interface Redesign

UI/UX Design
2020

Tool: Sketch, Adobe Illustrator

Sany is a heavy equipment manufacturer with a presence in countries around the world.
In this project, I redesigned 2 key interfaces of a control panel, improving visual hierarchy and aesthetics. Delivered 3 visually captivating high-fidelity UI mockup proposals with distinct styles using Sketch and Adobe Illustrator. Provided assets to the development team - resulting in a 20% reduction in user errors and a 30% increase in user satisfaction.
