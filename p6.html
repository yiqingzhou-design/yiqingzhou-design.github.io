String.

Interactive Installation,
Product Design
2021

Tool: Pygame, Python, Lazer Cutting, Arduino, Rhino

String is an educational musical installation featuring a traditional Chinese instrument designed for the museums in order to explore the ways in which music from different cultures could be demonstrated using sonic interaction.
