Escape.

Game Design
2022

Tool: P5.js, P5.play, Procreate

Escape is an adventure game. The goal is to escape from the building by making the right choice at the right time. It is all about timing. Players can press the button “space” on the keyboard to go back to 20 seconds before.
This is a game prototype made within 10 hours.
