Fold
World.

Interactive Installation
Game Design
2023

Tool: Teachable Machine, P5.js

FoldWorld is an innovative AI-powered AR game that embodies the fun of paper folding. It provides a tangible play experience that combines the tactile interaction of board games with the dynamic experience of video games, allowing players to interact with the projected interface by folding papers. FoldWorld demonstrates the practical application of machine learning to game design, highlighting the potential of this interaction mechanism in various scenarios, including gaming and STEM education.
