Yin.

Industrial Design
2021

Tool: Openpose, Python, Arduino, Rhino, Cinema 4D, Sketch

Yin is a motion-sensing fitness game for the elderly featuring Mawangdui Daoyin Exercises —— originated from the Pictures of Daoyin Exercises unearthed from the Mawangdui Tombs of the Han Dynasty(206 BC—AD 220) in Hunan, China.
